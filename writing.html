Writing plays an important role in the development of Clare's own work. Repetition of the spoken or sung word often features in her performance works.

She writes catalogue essays and reviews for the UK regional press and international art journals. Some of her art writing can be read on her writing archive web-site www.seesee-write.com. In spring 2016 she launched IRIS (© Clare Carswell), an online audio art review platform with colleagues in the US and Ireland. irisartinsights.tumblr.com

She is currently researching the first of a planned series of books, 'The Red Thread Series' (© Clare Carswell), which examines the imaginary dialogue between artists living and dead, and explores the intellectual lineage of established artists' works.

Clare writes poetry and is writing her first novel.
